# FUTURE_DS_01 - Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Objective

Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, high-value categories, and regional performance.

## Tools Used

* Google Sheets
* Pivot Tables
* Charts and Dashboard Visualization

## Dataset

The dataset contains sales transaction records with the following fields:

* Order Date
* Product
* Category
* Region
* Quantity
* Sales

## Analysis Performed

### 1. Revenue Trend Analysis

Analyzed monthly sales revenue to identify growth patterns and peak-performing periods.

### 2. Top-Selling Products

Identified products contributing the highest revenue.

### 3. High-Value Categories

Compared sales across product categories to determine the most profitable category.

### 4. Regional Performance

Evaluated revenue generated by different regions.

## Key Findings

* Laptop was the top-selling product.
* Electronics was the highest revenue-generating category.
* West region achieved the highest sales performance.
* Revenue peaked during April 2025.

## Dashboard Components

* Revenue Trend Line Chart
* Top-Selling Products Bar Chart
* Category-wise Sales Pie Chart
* Regional Performance Column Chart

## Recommendations

* Increase inventory of high-performing products.
* Focus marketing efforts on high-revenue regions.
* Introduce promotional campaigns for low-performing categories.
* Monitor monthly trends to improve sales forecasting.

## Outcome

Successfully created a business sales analytics dashboard that provides insights into revenue trends, product performance, category contribution, and regional sales performance.
